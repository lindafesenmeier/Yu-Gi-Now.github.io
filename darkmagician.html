About Yu-Gi-Now!

Yu-Gi-Oh! is a world famous franchise with a tv-show and a card game that is played by millions of people around the world. The game consists of thousands of cards and it is therefore impossible to remember all the cards. In order to make the game more convenient to play or to understand the cards better in general, especially for the younger players, Yu-Gi-Now! has been created.
